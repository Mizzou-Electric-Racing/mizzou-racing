Business & Finances

What We Do...

On the business and finance side of the team, members work to ensure the success of our team by managing the budget, maintaining relationships with sponsors, and presenting the business and cost events at competition. The work we do supports the endeavors of the engineering subteams and allows them to complete their projects on time and on budget.

Treasurer: Justin Bowers

Justin is a second-year Computer Science and Electrical Engineering student. He joined the team in Fall 2022 after he saw a flyer hanging outside of Naka Hall. Justin manages the budget, tracks team spending, designs electrical harnesses and embedded software systems, and considers himself the “resident CAN (theory) expert.”
