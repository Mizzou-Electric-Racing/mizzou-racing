Powertrain

The powertrain consists of an EMRAX 228 axial flux motor, a CM200 inverter, and a battery pack made of 805 Li-ion 18650 cells in a 115s 7p configuration. The system has a capacity of over 8 kWh, and at max charge, reaches nearly 500 volts. Our team wires and integrates these components in house.
