Our first EV! Car #237 made it to Michigan after the hard work from many team members. This, in itself, is a huge accomplishment for the team. We couldn't have done it without graduated members Spencer Goldstein, Cory Haberberger, & Chris Drake and Mizzou Electric Racing founders Hayden Higgins, Brooke Becker, & Caleb Hollenbeck. Their legacy will be remembered and honored by current & future Mizzou Racers.
